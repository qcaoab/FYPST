How to use

You can upload your own style image and transfer your photo to that style by following the instructions below:

  • Click on the first grey “Choose File” button to open your file explorer, and select your content image whose style is to be transferred from your local disks
  • Click on the second grey “Choose File” button to upload your style images whose styles will be transferred to your content image (Note that you can upload more than one style image and the resulting image will try to combine all these styles together.)
  • Then slide through the slide bar to select the desired extent of stylization (If the value is closer to 0, the image will be more similar to the original one and vice versa.)
  • Next, indicate whether you want to preserve the color of the original content image during the transfer process
  • Finally, click the “Submit” button to start the transfer process
