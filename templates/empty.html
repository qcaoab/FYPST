Introduction

Since the dawn of human history, human beings have formed the tendency to appreciate beauty. The aesthetic pursuit of human nature inspires the development of art. As a result, innumerable masterpieces of artwork were born in history and were admired by people until now. Nevertheless, the creation of such artworks can never be easy, as it requires not only the professional skills of the artists but also a large amount of time. Therefore, how to create artworks in a more efficient way has aroused the interest of modern researchers. Fortunately, as deep learning techniques developed, a solution with Neural Style Transfer (NST) was provided.

In this project, we are implementing a Neural Style Transfer system that can generate new images of artworks in two ways.

For the first one, you can upload two images, namely, a photo-like content image and a painting-like style reference image. By performing deep learning algorithms, the system will extract the artistic style from the style reference image and apply it to the content image, so that the output image will have the same content as the content image but painted in the style as the style reference image. To explore more details about this functionality, navigate through the top menu bar and click the “UPLOAD” button.

For the second one, we have built a template art gallery from which you can choose pre-defined artistic styles, such as Van Gogh’s style, Monet’s style, Japanese ukiyoe style, and so on. What you need to do is simply upload your own content image and freely choose from one of these popular styles to generate your transferred image as desired. To explore more details about this functionality, navigate through the top menu bar and click the “GALLARY” button.

By using the Neural Style Transfer technique provided on this website, everyone can create their own masterpieces in just a few seconds without special skills requirements. And this is totally free of charge. So, feel free to play around with all of the functionalities and enjoy yourselves. If you have any question and find any problem, please do no hesitate to contact us.
